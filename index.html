The Digital Experience Platform (DXP) Journey Creator component is easy to implement and configure, and interfaces effortlessly with existing back-end systems.


Its intuitive “drag and drop” dashboard radically simplifies digital journey building allowing staff with no developer or coding experience to quickly and easily create, configure and launch simple, frictionless, end-to-end digital customer journeys, across multiple engagement channels.
This e-Learning module and other resources will familiarize users with the concepts behind DXP and how to create web applications in Journey Creator.
